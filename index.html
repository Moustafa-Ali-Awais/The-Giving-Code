About "The Giving Code"

The Giving Code combines technology and compassion to inspire positive change. This platform was built from scratch, not using any ready-made templates, to bring our unique vision to life.

About the Creators

We are Moustafa and Ahmed, the creators of this website. Inspired by ALX's motto "Do Hard Things," we aim to guide future leaders and empower them with the lessons we've learned. This project is our way of giving back and inspiring others to create change.

Why Choose Us?

Over the past three years, we've faced numerous challenges and have grown immensely through ALX's teachings. We understand your struggles and have dedicated ourselves to making a difference. If we can create change, so can you.
